Velvet Elegance - Beef Stroganoff

Savor the Elegance, Satisfy the Soul

Dive into culinary luxury with our Velvet Elegance Beef Stroganoff. This classic dish marries tender beef, earthy mushrooms, and a creamy sauce, creating a symphony of flavors that is both comforting and sophisticated. Whether it's a special occasion or a cozy night in, this dish promises pure satisfaction in every bite

Ingredients

  • 1 pound beef sirloin, thinly sliced
  • 8 oz mushrooms, sliced
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup beef broth
  • 1/2 cup sour cream
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon Dijon mustard
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish
  • Cooked egg noodles or rice for serving

Steps

  1. Sear the Beef:
    • Heat olive oil and 1 tablespoon of butter in a skillet over medium-high heat
    • Add the sliced beef and cook until browned. Remove from the skillet and set aside

  2. Sauté the Aromatics:
    • In the same skillet, add the remaining tablespoon of butter
    • Add chopped onion and garlic, sauté until softened and fragrant

  3. Add Mushrooms:
    • Add the sliced mushrooms and cook until they release their moisture and become browned

  4. Make the Sauce:
    • Sprinkle the flour over the mushroom mixture and stir to combine
    • Pour in the beef broth, sour cream, Dijon mustard, and paprika
    • Stir well and simmer until the sauce thickens

  5. Return Beef to Skillet:
    • Return the seared beef to the skillet
    • Simmer for a few more minutes to heat the beef through
    • Season with salt and pepper to taste

  6. Serve:
    • Serve the Beef Stroganoff over cooked egg noodles or rice
    • Garnish with fresh parsley

  7. Enjoy:
    • Indulge in the sumptuous flavors of Velvet Elegance Beef Stroganoff, where each bite is an invitation to culinary elegance and satisfaction.
